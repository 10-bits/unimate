  <h1 align="center">Unimate</h1>

  <p align="center">
    Unimate is an ongoing research collaboration between Robert Gordon University (RGU), Aberdeen, Scotland and the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), Sri Lanka. The purpose of this study is to develop a digital intervention (app) aimed at enhancing studentsâ€™ educational experience and wellbeing. The research team, in consultation with IIT students, have identified a need for an app that helps students to manage their studies and also provides information, advice and signposting on physical and mental wellbeing.
